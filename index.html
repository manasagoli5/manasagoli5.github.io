Alcohol Detection and Vehicle Controlling This project developed a prototype alcohol detection and engine locking system by using an Arduino microcontroller interfaced with an alcohol sensor along with an LCD screen and a DC motor to demonstrate the concept. By placing the alcohol sensor on the steering wheel in the car, our system has the capacity to continuously perform the checking operations on alcohol level from the driver’s breath. If the driver is drunk while driving, the sensor will detect the alcohol content in his breath and stop the engine.
